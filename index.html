About us

Tosh Monsey

Kahilos Tosh-Monsey , a Torah kingdom of worldwide acclaim, is a leading educational network with highly recognized achievements. Essentially unassuming, this magnificent empire has gained respect and fame throughout the Torah world for its high scholastic standard and significant contributions to the furtherance of Torah study. Tosh-Monsey was founded by the revered Grand Rabbi of Tosh ztz”l. Due to his high regard and deep-rooted trust in the capabilities of his esteemed son-in-law, Harav Hagaon Reb Avrohom Katz shlit"a, the Rebbe apoointed him to lead the institutions of Tosh Monsey. Under his leadership, the yeshiva and kolelim have become the prototype for establishments of serious Torah learning.

Named by the venerable Rebbe ztz”l to indicate its purpose, Mishkanos Lavir Yakov truly provides dwelling places for Hashem. The yeshiva and kolelim, and the homes that its alumni have built and will build in the future, are places where the divine presence resides and from which it finds pleasure. Those who have the opportunity to observe the spiritual beauty of these holy "Mishkanos" are awed by its unique atmosphere. The yeshiva bachurim and kolel members that are fortunate to serve Hashem between its walls are Torah students with tremendous potential. They eagerly delve into the depths of the Talmud and Shulchan Aruch, amassing extensive Talmudic and Halachic knowledge, and becoming the future Torah leaders and talmidei chachamim of our nation.

Huruah

The Rabbi shlit"a is a capable leader and a brilliant general of his prestigious Torah-force. His vast experience in spreading Torah and guiding young men earned him the respect and devotion of hundreds of students. the Rov dedicated himself entirely to bringing out the best of the young men in the community. His efforts bore beautiful fruit, and many of his students are today exceptional talmidei chachamim, and people with a deep commitment to Torah and divine service.

Kollel

Kollel Avreichim-Monsey

The Kolel in Monsey is the perfect setting where a select group of diligent and serious young men can advance in their studies and make progress in their learning. From early until well into the evening, these promising scholars apply themselves fully to their studies. The Rabbi shlit"a has strong connection with the kolel, guiding the the young me towards achieving greatness.
